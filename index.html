üíπWhat is AV token?

   In addition, we have launched the AV token and established an initial liquidity pool on the STONfi DeFi platform. At early stages it will be used as native marketplace trading token to support the overall capitalization of the project, offering a number of privileges for the .///HEAVEN community members.

   The neurocontainer ownership will bring benefits on "basic income" modifier launch. This means that by purchasing and holding a neurocontainer, in the result you will earn a certain number of AV tokens based on blockchain data, even if you are no longer own the NFT.

   Unlocking of tokens into DeFi pool will be managed according to the increase in demand, to avoid a collapse in capitalization and the accumulation of large volumes of tokens in the hands of individuals interested in malicious actions.

ü™Ç Airdrop

   Currently, the only way to participate in the token distribution is by purchasing a neurocontainer. We have concluded that recent methods of community developing, such as massive token giveaways and clickers, are not suitable for our project.

   First of all, we are interested in building an engaged community and a strong tokenomics. The random token giveaways could lead to a decrease in the general value and overall disappointment which we want to avoid.
